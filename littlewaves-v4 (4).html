OUR STORY

BEGAN WITH A SIMPLE BELIEF

Every child learns best when swimming lessons are flexible, personal, and fun. We created a swim school that comes to families, adapts to each swimmer's pace, and builds confidence without pressure.

We noticed how traditional swim programs often follow rigid schedules and crowded classes, leaving children and parents feeling rushed or left behind. So we decided to create something different: a swim school that comes to you, adapts to your goals, and makes learning feel natural and stress-free.

With certified, safety-first coaches and convenient lesson bundles, LittleWaves brings structured yet adaptable training to every family. Whether it's building water confidence, improving technique, or preparing for SwimSafer certification, we meet swimmers where they are and help them grow at their own pace.

Like small waves that grow stronger with each ripple, we believe every child can build confidence, skill, and joy one swim at a time.

CANCELLATION POLICY

At LittleWaves, we truly understand that life happens — plans change, the weather surprises us, and little swimmers sometimes fall ill. While we always do our best to stay flexible, we also ask for your kind understanding that some circumstances are beyond our control.

To keep lessons fair for both families and instructors, our policy is as follows:

  • Rescheduling or cancellations should be made at least 2 hours before your scheduled lesson.
  • Up to 2 cancellations due to bad weather, facility closure, or with a valid medical certificate (MC) will not result in a lost lesson credit.
  • Discretionary cancellations: 1 session (with at least 1-day prior notice).
  • Medical cancellations: 2 sessions (valid MC submitted within 3 days).
  • No-shows or unjustified absences will result in the credit for that session being forfeited.
  • Group lessons will continue as planned for other swimmers; absentees will still be charged in full.

Make-up sessions may be offered at the instructor's goodwill and availability, but we appreciate your patience and understanding that this may not always be possible due to scheduling limits.

We care deeply about every swimmer's progress — thank you for helping us keep lessons running smoothly for everyone. 🏊
